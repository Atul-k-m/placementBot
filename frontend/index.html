Security & Privacy Guarantee

Your credentials are meant to stay yours. When you link your Gmail or WhatsApp API tokens, we automatically hash and encrypt them using industry-standard AES-256 GCM encryption at rest. Passwords are mathematically salted and hashed with bcrypt. No credential is ever logged or exposed in plaintext.
