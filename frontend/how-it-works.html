3 Steps to Inbox Zen

Step 1: Connect Gmail

Generate a local OAuth token from Google Cloud Console. Paste the raw JSON securely into our dashboard. We immediately encrypt the token using AES-256 so that it remains protected and unreadable in the database.

Step 2: Add Senders

Specify the exact email addresses you want monitored (like support@apple.com, hr@company.com). The bot will ignore everything else.

Step 3: Get WhatsApp Alerts

Register your phone number with the free CallMeBot API. Set your desired notification time. Every day at your scheduled time, the bot securely fetches the emails and sends a beautiful digest directly to your native WhatsApp.
